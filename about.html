From Bait to Brews

It all started in 1982. Back then, this spot was nothing more than "Old Man Miller's Bait Shop"‚Äîa wooden shack where locals picked up shiners before heading out onto the chain of lakes.

Over the years, folks started hanging around after their fishing trips, sharing stories about the "one that got away" over cold beers from the cooler. We realized that while the bait was important, the stories were even better.

In 2010, The Twisted Prop was officially born. We kept the rustic charm (and a few of the old fishing nets), but traded the bait tanks for a full kitchen and the cooler for a fully stocked bar. Today, we're proud to be Winter Haven's favorite spot to watch the sunset, listen to local music, and keep the tradition of big fish stories alive.
