The FAQ

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis. However, we do take call-aheads for parties of 10 or more!

Are dogs allowed?

Absolutely! Well-behaved pups are welcome on our outdoor patio. We even have water bowls ready.

Do you have boat slips?

Yes, we have 5 slips available for guests arriving by water. Please monitor channel 16 upon approach.

Is there live music every night?

We feature live local talent every Friday and Saturday night, and Sunday afternoons. Check our events calendar!
